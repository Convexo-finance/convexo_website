Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our cross-border payment solutions.

How quickly are cross-border payments processed?

Our cross-border payments are processed in seconds, with most transactions completing within 3-5 seconds regardless of destination. This is significantly faster than traditional bank transfers which can take 3-5 business days.

What currencies do you support?

We support over 50 fiat currencies and major digital assets. Our platform handles currency conversion automatically at competitive rates, allowing you to send and receive payments in the currency of your choice.

How do you ensure transaction security?

Security is our top priority. We implement bank-grade encryption, multi-factor authentication, and blockchain-based verification for all transactions. Our platform is audited regularly by third-party security firms, and we maintain compliance with international financial regulations.

What are the fees for cross-border payments?

Our fee structure is transparent and simple: we charge a flat 0.5% fee for most transactions, with volume discounts available for businesses with high transaction volumes. Unlike traditional banks, we don't add hidden fees or markup the exchange rates significantly.

How does the API integration work?

Our RESTful API allows you to integrate our payment infrastructure directly into your existing systems. We provide comprehensive documentation, SDKs for popular programming languages, and a sandbox environment for testing. Most businesses can complete the integration within days rather than weeks.

Is there a minimum transaction amount?

We have no minimum transaction amount for standard accounts. For high-volume enterprise accounts, we offer custom pricing and features tailored to your specific business needs regardless of individual transaction sizes.
