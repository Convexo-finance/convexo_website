How It Works

Our streamlined process makes payroll management effortless and error-free.

1

Employee Onboarding

Seamlessly add employees to your payroll system with our intuitive onboarding process. Each employee receives a secure digital wallet linked to their company identity.

2

Configure Payment Settings

Set up payment schedules, currencies, and distribution rules. Define salary components, deductions, and saving plans based on your company policies.

3

Fund Your Payroll Account

Deposit funds into your corporate account in your preferred currency or digital asset. Our system ensures optimal liquidity and exchange rates.

4

Review and Approve

Validate the payroll cycle with our advanced reconciliation tools. Multi-level approvals ensure accuracy and compliance with your authorization policies.

5

Instant Distribution

With a single click, execute the entire payroll cycle. Funds are instantly distributed to all employee wallets, with real-time confirmation and detailed reporting.
