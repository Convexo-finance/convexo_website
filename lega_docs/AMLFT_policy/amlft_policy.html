Anti-Money Laundering & Counter-Terrorism Financing Policy

Last Updated: April 2024

CONVEXO FINANCE LIMITED is committed to preventing and combating money laundering and terrorist financing. This policy outlines our procedures and measures to ensure compliance with relevant regulations and maintain the integrity of the global financial system.

1. Introduction and Legal Framework

This Anti-Money Laundering and Counter-Terrorism Financing (AML/CFT) Policy establishes the principles and minimum standards that must be adhered to across all operations of CONVEXO FINANCE LIMITED.

We are committed to complying with all applicable AML/CFT laws, regulations, and industry standards in the jurisdictions where we operate, including but not limited to:

  • Financial Action Task Force (FATF) Recommendations
  • Local AML/CFT legislation
  • Regulatory requirements specific to digital asset service providers

2. Know Your Customer (KYC) Procedures

CONVEXO FINANCE LIMITED implements robust KYC procedures to verify the identity of all clients:

For Individuals:

  • Government-issued photo identification verification
  • Proof of address verification (utility bills, bank statements)
  • Source of funds declaration
  • Politically Exposed Person (PEP) screening

For Businesses:

  • Business registration and incorporation documents
  • Corporate structure and Ultimate Beneficial Owner (UBO) identification
  • Verification of authorized representatives
  • Business activity assessment

3. Risk Assessment and Management

We employ a risk-based approach to AML/CFT compliance, assessing and categorizing clients and transactions based on various risk factors:

Client Risk Factors:

  • Geographic location and jurisdiction
  • Business activities and industry
  • Political exposure
  • Transaction patterns and volumes

Transaction Risk Factors:

  • Source and destination of funds
  • Transaction size and frequency
  • Transaction patterns inconsistent with client profile
  • Involvement of high-risk jurisdictions

Based on these assessments, we apply enhanced due diligence measures for higher-risk clients and transactions.

4. Transaction Monitoring

CONVEXO FINANCE LIMITED conducts ongoing monitoring of client transactions to detect and report suspicious activities:

  • Automated systems to identify unusual transaction patterns
  • Regular review of high-value and high-risk transactions
  • Screening against sanctions lists and adverse media
  • Periodic review of client profiles and risk assessments

5. Suspicious Activity Reporting

We maintain procedures for internal reporting of suspicious activities and external reporting to relevant authorities:

  • Clear internal escalation protocols
  • Prompt investigation of potential suspicious activities
  • Timely filing of Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) with appropriate authorities
  • Confidentiality measures to protect the reporting process

6. Sanctions Compliance

CONVEXO FINANCE LIMITED ensures compliance with international and local sanctions regimes:

  • Regular screening of clients and transactions against sanctions lists
  • Blocking of transactions involving sanctioned individuals or entities
  • Ongoing monitoring of changes to sanctions regimes
  • Implementation of controls to prevent sanctions evasion

7. Record Keeping

We maintain comprehensive records of client identification, transactions, and compliance activities:

  • Retention of all KYC documentation for at least 5 years
  • Secure storage of transaction records
  • Documentation of compliance decisions and actions
  • Audit trail of all AML/CFT activities

8. Staff Training and Awareness

CONVEXO FINANCE LIMITED ensures that all employees receive appropriate AML/CFT training:

  • Comprehensive onboarding training for new employees
  • Regular refresher training for all staff
  • Specialized training for compliance personnel
  • Updates on emerging risks and regulatory changes

9. Independent Audit and Review

Our AML/CFT program is subject to regular independent review and testing:

  • Periodic internal compliance reviews
  • Regular independent third-party audits
  • Timely remediation of identified deficiencies
  • Reporting of audit results to senior management and board
