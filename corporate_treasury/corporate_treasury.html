Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our corporate treasury solution.

How does multisig wallet security work?

Our multisignature wallet technology requires multiple authorized signers to approve transactions before execution. You define the number of signatures required (e.g., 3-of-5, 2-of-3) and assign authorized signers within your organization. This ensures no single point of failure or unauthorized access, even if a signer's credentials are compromised.

Can we customize approval workflows for different transaction types?

Yes, our platform allows you to create different approval workflows based on transaction size, asset type, destination, and purpose. For example, you can set up a 2-of-3 approval process for routine transactions under $10,000, while requiring 4-of-5 approvals for transactions over $100,000 or for specific asset types that may carry higher risk.

How are treasury assets secured?

We implement multiple layers of security, including cold storage for the majority of assets, MPC (Multi-Party Computation) technology, hardware security modules, and comprehensive encryption. All critical operations require multi-factor authentication, and our systems undergo regular security audits by independent third parties. Additionally, all assets are covered by our comprehensive insurance policy.

Can we integrate the treasury solution with our existing financial systems?

Yes, our treasury solution offers comprehensive API access for integration with your existing financial systems, ERP software, and accounting platforms. We also provide pre-built connectors for popular systems like SAP, Oracle, and NetSuite. Our professional services team can assist with custom integrations to ensure seamless data flow and reconciliation.

How long does it take to set up a corporate treasury solution?

Typical implementation time ranges from 2-4 weeks, depending on the complexity of your requirements and the level of customization needed. Our implementation process includes security setup, governance configuration, access control definition, user training, and optional system integrations. We provide dedicated implementation specialists to ensure a smooth and efficient onboarding experience.

What types of digital assets are supported?

Our treasury solution supports a wide range of digital assets, including major cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.), stablecoins (USDC, USDT, etc.), and tokenized securities. We regularly add support for additional assets based on market demand and our rigorous security assessment process. Custom asset support can be implemented upon request.
